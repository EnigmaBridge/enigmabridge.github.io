Your Key Generation Methods are visible to Everyone!

Our co-founder, Petr Svenda, just released a paper on his team's discovery that https encryption keys leak information on how they were generated. This suprising discovery can put companies at risk as it gives out information on their cybersecurity management, which to date has been considered a secret.

"It's striking that despite 30 odd years of cryptographic research and security conferences, no-one noticed this problem – which has been in plain sight all along."
Ross Anderson, prof. at University of Cambridge



Enigma Bridge has expanded on the discovery and built a tool that can with great accuracy tell what security mechanisms companies use for their https key generation. Send us an email we’ll prove it by telling you how your own key generation looks like and how secure it is.
